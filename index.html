dhime

Building an AI & Computer Vision based product that brings ease & true convenience to the hobby learning. Learn Dance and Music spontaneously from anywhere at anytime without a prior schedule.

Unlimited Guided Practice

Practice under guidance is the single most important part of learning dance & music. In addition to the dance tutorials, Dhime offers unlimited dance practice with instant feedback and guidance.

Spontaneous Learning

The teachers and the learners no longer need to pre-plan the learning schedule. The teachers prepare the dance or music tutorials in their free time. The learners start grooving or strumming spontaneously as and when they feel like learning.

Personalized Learning

Gone are the days when every learner in the group learns the same dance sequences in the same order at the same pace. Dhime personalizes the dance & music lessons to each individual learner.

Affordability and Scalability

True democratization of the hobby learning is only possible when it is affordable and scalable to the everyone in the world. Affordability and Scalability form the bedrock of the conceptulization and development at Dhime.
