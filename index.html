How It Works

Step 0

Tutors prepare video lessons of the dance sequences. And make them available on Dhime to the students worldwide. Each video lesson contains detailed explanatations, dos & don'ts.

Step 1

A student visits Dhime website or opens the Dhime App. Watches a dance lesson and understands it.

Step 2

The student tries the dance himself. After trying for few times, he decides to ask feeback from the Dhime AI.

Step 3

Student asks for feedback in the App and performs the dance in front of the camera. The camera records the student dance and the Dhime AI provides instant feedback by analysing the recording.

Step 4

The student sees the feedback. It includes a simple one line statement on how to improve/correct himself and also a detailed comprehensive feedback. The student continues with his practice by improving himself based on the feedback.
