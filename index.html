dhime

Building AI solutions that bring the quality extracurricular education to the reach of the 7 Billion citizens of the world.

Unlimited Guided Practice

Practice under guidance is the single most important component of learning. Dhime is building solutions that not just lecture but also offer unlimited practice with instant feedback and guidance.

Anytime & Anyplace

Aspiration is all it takes. Well rounded education is no longer limited to few places of the world. And it is no longer limited by the student's ability to be there on time. Students can choose their time and place to learn.

Individual Focus on Every Student

Gone are the days when every student has to learn from the same course in the same order at the same pace. Dhime is building solutions that tune the content, sequence and pace uniquely to each individual learner.

Affordability and Scalability

True democratization of the education is possible only when the learning is affordable and scalable to the everyone in the world. These two principles form the bedrock of the conception and development of every solution at Dhime.
