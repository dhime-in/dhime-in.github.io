No other Personally Identifiable Information is used or accessed by the Applications. Even your recorded dance video is processed locally on the phone and is deleted immediately after the analysis. It is never sent out of the phone.

These Non Personal Identification Information include City, Region & Country, to personalize the content to you. It also includes any software crash logs, stack traces and software diagnostics, to help us identify and fix any software issues. It also include your interactions with the applications and usage of various parts of the applications to help understand the portions of the applications that need improvement.

All the above Non Personal Identifiable Information is used in an anonymous fashion without attributing any of these to any single user, but using them in an overall statistical way.
